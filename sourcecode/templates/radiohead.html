Radiohead

OK COMPUTER • 1997 • Alternative Rock

NME Magazine Rating:

Album Background

This was the third studio album created by Radiohead. OK COMPUTER reached number 1 in the UK charts despite lower sales being estimated. This has now been named as one of the greatest albums ever made.
