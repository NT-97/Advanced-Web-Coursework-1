Coldplay

Mylo Xyloto • 2011 • Pop/Electronic

NME Magazine Rating:

Album Background

Fifth studio album created by Coldplay who are of British origin. Mylo Xyloto is a concept album and thematic rock opera. the album tells the story of a war against sound and colour by a supremacist government, set in the world of silencia, an orwellian society. The album received mixed to positive reviews from the music critics.
