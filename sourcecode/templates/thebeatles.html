Album Background

Abbey Road is a rock album that incorporates genres such as blues, pop and progressive rock. Although Abbey Road was an immediate commercial success and reached number one in the UK and US, it initially received mixed reviews, some critics describing its music as inauthentic and bemoaning the productions artificial effects.
