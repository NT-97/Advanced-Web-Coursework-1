Album Background

Greatest Hits is a compilation album by the British rock band Queen, released worldwide on 26 Ocotber 1981. The album consisted of Queens best selling singles since their first chart appearance in 1974. there was no universal track listing or cover art for the album, and each territory's tracks were dependent on what singles had been released there and which were successful.
